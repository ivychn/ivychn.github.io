    ORIGINS/
    When we dissociate, when we break free from our daily stressors and rituals, when we stop keeping our head down and instead allow ourselves to freely think about anything and everything, is that who we are meant to be? How much of ourselves do we actually understand? Do we live in a fantasy that cannot be captured by a full length film? No matter how much we develop artifical intelligence alongside the permutations of science, will these models ever be able to replicate the real, free-thinking, vivacious beings of a soul? (Where do souls go after their host's brain is pronounced dead?)
    These albums contain songs that make me think a lot. A lot about my life and also about nothing. I feel like the state of being able to think about absolutely nothing and everything at the same time is such a trip reality which I absolutely adore. Like it's beyond fascinating in my opinion to be able to just be in that natural flow state without much prompting besides the right playlist and hitting the play button.
